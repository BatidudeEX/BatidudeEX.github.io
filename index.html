About Me

Hi, I'm Jeven Shirrell, a 15-year-old high school freshman who loves music and computer programming. I have been developing video games for the past 4 years now, and I've even started my own freelance shop on Fiverr where I make custom games for people. My musical journey started in 2019 when I started making my own songs on an iPad. This evolved over the past 5 years to where I am now using my laptop and MIDI devices to make my own beats that I market through another separate Fiverr shop. I also play drums for my band called Three Heart Cross, which writes a mix of Christian and secular music in a wide range of genres. We have opened up for some pretty big names, such as Spoken and Disciple.
